Security Checklist

  • Strong flashlight with strobe mode
  • To light up dark areas and disorient threats at night if needed.
  • Whistle and personal alarm
  • Noise tools to call for help or scare off people and animals.
  • Locks and cables
  • Padlocks and cable locks for doors, gates, bikes, and gear.
  • Basic protective gear
  • Sturdy gloves, safety glasses, and a hard hat for debris and barricades.
  • Awareness plan
  • Printed contacts, safe places marked on maps, and a simple movement plan.
