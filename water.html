Hydration Checklist

  • Purified bottled water (bulk cases)
  • Stackable cases of sealed drinking water for at least 30 days.
  • Large water storage containers
  • Food-grade barrels or jugs with lids for long-term storage at home.
  • Portable water filters
  • Backpack-style or straw filters for rivers, lakes, or unknown sources.
  • Water purification tablets
  • Chemical tablets for backup when filters break or water is very dirty.
  • Collapsible water bags
  • Lightweight containers I can fold, pack, and fill only when needed.
