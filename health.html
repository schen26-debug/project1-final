Health Checklist

  • First aid kit
  • Bandages, gauze, tape, scissors, antiseptic wipes, and a first aid manual.
  • Common medicines
  • Pain relievers, fever reducers, allergy medicine, stomach medicine, and prescriptions.
  • Masks and gloves
  • Disposable masks and nitrile gloves for wounds and unknown air.
  • Toothbrush, toothpaste, and soap
  • Basic hygiene that prevents infection and keeps me feeling human.
  • Toilet paper and trash bags
  • Waste control when normal systems fail, plus sealable bags for dirty items.
