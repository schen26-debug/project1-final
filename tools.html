Important: A small tool at the right time can save your life.

Tools Checklist

  • Flashlights and headlamps
  • Bright lights with extra batteries or hand-crank recharge options.
  • Multi-tool or basic toolkit
  • Knife, pliers, screwdrivers, and other essentials in a compact kit.
  • Fire starters
  • Waterproof matches, lighters, and solid fuel fire starter blocks.
  • Duct tape and paracord
  • For patching, tying, hanging, building, and improvising almost anything.
  • Portable power bank
  • High-capacity battery and, if possible, a small solar charger.
