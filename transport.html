Important: When staying is no longer safe, you must move.

Transportation Checklist

  • Primary vehicle kit
  • Car in good condition with extra fuel, jumper cables, and tools.
  • Bug-out bag
  • Backpack with water, food, clothes, and essentials ready to grab.
  • Alternative transport
  • Bike, folding scooter, or similar if roads are blocked or fuel is gone.
  • Printed maps
  • Paper maps of my city and nearby areas in case GPS and phones fail.
