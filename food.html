Food Checklist

  • Dry staples (rice, pasta, oats)
  • Large bags that last for months and cook with simple heat and water.
  • Canned goods
  • Beans, vegetables, soups, and meat that I can eat hot or cold.
  • Freeze-dried or dehydrated meals
  • Lightweight packs that only need hot water and store easily.
  • High-energy snacks
  • Energy bars, nuts, peanut butter, chocolate, and trail mix.
  • Cooking basics
  • Salt, oil, spices, sugar, and instant coffee or tea.
