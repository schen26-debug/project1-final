Shelter

Shelter protects you from the cold, the weather, and the things that wander in the night. This page are the tools you need to build a barrier between you and the outside world.

Important: Prevent cold and wet.

Shelter Checklist

  • Sturdy tent or tarp shelter
  • Durable tent or heavy tarp with ropes and stakes for fast cover.
  • Sleeping bag and emergency blankets
  • Cold-rated bag plus foil blankets to hold body heat.
  • Insulating clothing layers
  • Waterproof jacket, gloves, hat, and extra socks in a dry bag.
  • Portable stove and fuel
  • Small camping stove and fuel canisters for boiling water and heating food.
